Overview of the Project

The project title 'SEClms' is the abbreviation of 'Library Management System of Sylhet Engineering College'. It is an Web application which is made for the students and the Librarian of the Sylhet Engineering College. The project 'SEClms' is developed in python based on python Framework 'django', which mainly focuses on basic operations in a library.Here, Librarian and Students can search for the books, students, borrow informationâ€™s and can search for the today , delay and upcoming 7 days borrow information with the help of dynamic live search which is built with 'ajax'. The librarian can monitoring and controlling the transactions in a library. In Home view of the web app student can see that those who have to return books today , the upcoming 7 days return list and the fine for the students those who delayed to return the books. we can say that, the 'SEClms' is a live web app for the students and librarian. This web app provides facilities for not only the librarian but also for the students.

Facilities for the Librarian

Adding new books information.

Adding new students in the library.

Adding books type, shelf position and room numbers for the new insertion books.

Searching in insertion form for giving books to students. As, there are so many students and books , the librarian need to find out those who want to borrow books in a dynamically way.

Setting the fine for those who delay to return the books in time and can change the fine amount.

Searching in today returnlist , delay list and upcoming 7 days list. Librarian can easily find out whether a student is in fine list or not by live searching.

Automatic books quantity update when a book received or giving to a student.

Post a news in the news section.

Activity history for the librarian.

Theme changing for the web app.

Facilities for the Students

Students can see the today return list, delay list and upcoming 7 days list and also the information of books and students in the list by clicking on the student id or book name.

Studnets can Search the today return list , delay list and upcoming 7 days list and also the information of books and students in the list by clicking on the student id or books name. He/She can find out whether He/She in the delay list or not.

Students can see their fine for delaying of returning a books in time.

Students can search a books and see the details of the books whether the book is available in the library or not.The quantity of each book refers the availability of book.

Students can search themselves in student list and can see whether they are registered by the Librarian or not.

Students can search in whole borrow list and can see when they borrow books and also how much time is remained to return this books.

Students can discuss with librarian and also with others in an open discussion.

Students can see the news of librarian and can share their opinions.
